ABOUT US

Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering & Research (PCCOER), established in 2014 under the Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET), offers Undergraduate Programs in Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, E&TC Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by A.I.C.T.E., PCCOER follows Outcome Based Education and a Student-centric approach. With a Four-Pillar Philosophy—Academic Excellence, Research & Innovation, Professional Competence, and Social Commitment—PCCOER boasts qualified faculty, advanced infrastructure, and ranks among the top institutions in SPPU results. The institute focuses on research, innovation, patents, copyrights, incubation, and entrepreneurship, adopting Project Based Learning. Notably, PCCOER has set records for copyright applications and patents. The institute ensures excellent placements through various activities and training. Students benefit from diverse activities, including technical courses, AI, IoT, and robotics, alongside co-curricular and extra-curricular pursuits, cultural events, and a strong emphasis on national pride and ethics.
